I am currently an undergraduate senior at Stony Brook University, majoring in Computer Science with a specialization in Software Engineering.
I interned at Marvel Entertainment in 2018 where I helped write software used for processing batches of user data. In 2019 I interned at App Nexus (currently known as Xandr) for the Change Data Capture team where I helped write software that facilitated the streaming of data changes across the company. During late 2018-19 I spent time in MacCarthy's Computational Immunology Lab at the Stony Brook Laufer Center where I implemented algorithms for detecting and locating repeating mutations in immunoglobulin heavy chain genes. From early 2019-20 I spent lots of my free time in Stony Brook's Teaching Learning Lab (TLL/T) where I helped create software and websites used by undergraduate students. I also had the privilege of collaborating alongside graduate medical students during my time at the TLL.
